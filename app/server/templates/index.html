OgramCloud Beta

Freely Transform your TELEGRAM ACCOUNT into a CLOUD-STORAGE-SERVER without registration/tracking, NO LIMIT IN STORAGE.

OgramCoud is an Open Source test project made for FUN by Sanix-darker, -   Contributors list

How it works?

  1. Get your chatID from Ogram-Telegram-bot.
  2. Use it to save your files here, you will get a generated file-key !

    3. Your file is on Telegram-Servers now, using your account, make sure to always save your files's keys ('file-key') somewhere, you can share the download link.

  3. To regenerate your file, copy the file-key and generate the download link.

    4. All files are deleted each 24H after their download-link have been generated.

⚠︎ Because it's an experimental project on BETA version in a small server, for now you can just store only 100 Mo per upload.
